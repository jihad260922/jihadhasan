About Me

Hi! I’m Jihad Hasan, a passionate web designer and front-end developer with a strong love for creating visually stunning and interactive web experiences. My journey in web development began with curiosity for how websites work, and over time, I’ve transformed that curiosity into a deep expertise in designing and coding responsive, user-friendly websites.

I specialize in crafting modern, clean, and engaging designs, combining aesthetics with functionality to ensure every website I build is visually appealing and intuitive. My work includes personal portfolios, business websites, galleries, and interactive web applications that leave a lasting impression on users.

Beyond design and coding, I constantly explore new technologies, design trends, and creative tools to stay ahead in the fast-paced world of web development. I enjoy learning and applying creative solutions, animations, and smooth interactions that make websites feel alive.

When I’m not coding, I enjoy photography, exploring visual arts, and experimenting with UI/UX ideas, which often inspire the websites I create. My goal is to deliver not just websites, but complete digital experiences that reflect creativity, professionalism, and attention to detail.
